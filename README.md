üéØ **Business Case**
An HR department needed a single tool allowing it to manage all aspects of its personnel & compensation management. This dashboard replaced 5 Excel files. The objectives are to allow real-time monitoring of the workforce, their structure, overtime and the N-1 and N payroll in order to be able to manage the workforce planning, recruitments and to make informed decisions by greatly reducing budgetary unknowns and the lack of visibility in the medium/long term. 

üõ†Ô∏è **Power BI Desktop Skills (Analysis & Modeling)**
**Power Query:** Data cleaning via M (duplicate removal, pivoting, type handling), custom columns, query merge.
**Modeling :** Implementation of a star diagram with a fact table, optimized dimension tables, and a calendar table.
**DAX :** Creation of measures.
**Visuals :** Additional integrated visuals for better adaptation to the HR target.

‚òÅÔ∏è **Power BI Service Skills (Deployment & Governance)**

**Security:** Line security, role creation and differentiated sharing on PowerBi Service were also part of the training provided.

üìà **Key Insights & Results (examples)**
Observation 1: The report identified the months with the highest overtime rate.
Recommendation: Depending on the services and professions concerned, implement an annualization of working time to greatly reduce or even eliminate the use of overtime.  
Observation 2: Absenteeism could be precisely quantified in real time and especially upstream.
Recommendation: On very costly exceptional absence categories as a replacement for the payroll, better optimize workforce planning with secondments.
